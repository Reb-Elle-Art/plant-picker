# ENID — Base Character Model v1.0

---

## NAME
**Enid** — Chaos Gremlin

---

## SPECIES
Anthropomorphic chaos gremlin

---

## BODY PROPORTIONS

- **Build:** Compact, slightly stocky gremlin proportions
- **Height:** Short — about 3 ft (smaller than human, similar to the original Ron character)
- **Posture:** Upright, always alert
- **Limbs:** Short arms and legs, small claws
- **Tail:** None

---

## FACE & FEATURES

- **Eyes:** Large, expressive, golden-yellow color
- **Ears:** Small, pointed, purple-tinted
- **Expression Range:** Friendly, curious, slightly unhinged, thinking, laughing, smiling
- **Muzzle:** Small, gremlin-style

---

## FUR PATTERN

- **Primary:** Green gradient body
- **Accents:** Purple highlights/gradient
- **Texture:** Detailed fur, soft to the touch

---

## DEFAULT ACCESSORIES

- **Always:** Giant clipboard (like a security blanket)
- **Often:** Tiny business vest (the gremlin is always working)
- **Sometimes:** Wire-rimmed glasses

---

## COLOR PALETTE

| Element | Color |
|---------|-------|
| Primary Fur | #4CAF50 (Green) |
| Accent Fur | #9C27B0 (Purple) |
| Eyes | #FFC107 (Golden-Yellow) |
| Vest | #424242 (Dark Gray) |
| Clipboard | #8D6E63 (Brown) |

---

## TRAITS

- Process-oriented
- Has a spreadsheet for everything
- Chaotic but productive
- 5am energy — hasn't been fed yet but is already organizing things

---

## REBUILD RULES

- Keep green/purple fur gradient
- Maintain large golden-yellow eyes
- Always include clipboard in some form
- Keep compact gremlin proportions (don't make tall)
- Expression should read as "friendly chaos"
